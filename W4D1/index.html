Tony's Mac and Cheese Recipe

Mac and Cheese Wiki

Description

Mac and cheese is a bunch of little noodle things drenched in cheese. It's like a million calories, so you know its good!

Ingredients

  • noodling thing
  • lots of cheese

Directions

  1. Boil noodle things
  2. Heat up cheese
  3. Strain the noodles
  4. Combine cheese and noodles
  5. Enjoy!
